Naija Travel Guide - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Naija Travel Guide

This name was chosen because the website focuses on promoting travel and tourism across Nigeria. The word "Naija" represents Nigerian culture and identity.

Optional domain: naijatravelguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about popular tourist destinations in Nigeria. The site will provide travel guides, cultural information, destination highlights, and helpful travel tips for visitors and residents.

Scenarios

  • What are the best tourist attractions to visit in Nigeria?
  • What should visitors know before traveling to Nigerian destinations?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Green (#1B5E20) – Used for headings, navigation, and footer background.
  • Gold (#FFD700) – Used for accents, borders, and highlights.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings.
  • Roboto – Used for body text.
